End-to-end heat exchanger design from first principles, constrained by thermal performance, pressure drop, and geometric limits.

EGR(Exhaust Gas Recirculation)
EGR is used for the exhaust gas recirculation and cooling of engines. The portion of exhaust gas in the recirculation loop, due to its relatively high temperature, must pass through the EGR cooler for sufficient cooling. It then enters the cylinder together with the boosted air for combustion, effectively suppressing the formation of NOₓ, thus achieving energy-saving and emission-reduction effects.

The main body of the product is made of stainless steel, castings, and other metallic materials. Depending on the cooling medium used during operation, EGR coolers can be classified into water-cooled and air-cooled types. The product structure includes plate-fin type, tubular type, assembled components, and other structural types, featuring compact structure, diversity, high-temperature resistance, and good corrosion resistance.
